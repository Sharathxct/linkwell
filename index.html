Follow these simple steps to apply for loan
1
Filling Basic Details
Filling Basic Details
Filling out basic details is often the first step in applying for a loan or other financial service. The process is usually straightforward and can be completed very easily. Visit our office to begin the application process.
Checking eligibility
2
Checking eligibility
Once you have provided your basic details, the next step in the loan application process is to check your eligibility and review loan details. This step is crucial because it will help you understand whether you qualify for the loan and what the loan terms and conditions are.
3
Submitting Documents
Submitting Documents
Once you have reviewed the loan details and decided to proceed with the loan application, the next step is to submit the required documents. The documents required for a loan application vary depending on the type of loan and the lender's specific requirements.
Physical Verification
4
Physical Verification
After you have submitted the required documents, the next step in the loan application process is physical verification and discussion. This step involves a face-to-face meeting with our loan specialists to verify the information provided in your application and discuss any additional details or questions.
5
Loan Sanctioned
Loan Sanctioned
Once the physical verification and discussion step is complete, the next step in the loan application process is loan sanction. This step involves the lender reviewing your application and verifying that all the information provided is accurate and complete. If the lender is satisfied with the information provided, they will sanction the loan amount and inform you of the approval.
Loan Disbursement
6
Loan Disbursement
Once your loan is sanctioned and you have accepted the loan agreement, the final step in the loan application process is loan disbursement. This step involves the lender transferring the loan amount to your designated bank account.
